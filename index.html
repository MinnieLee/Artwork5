Coffee Making

Information

  • Content: Coffee Making
  • Size: 50 x 50 cm
  • Year:

    • Description

  • Time creates value. I chose the coffee pot, brewing tool, and grinder, classical media of making coffee for this art piece. It takes much longer time for one to make coffee this way; however, the coffee made through this method has unparalleled taste and aroma. The secret to its taste and aroma is, I think, time. The classical way of making coffee with its time-consuming method produces beautifully tasting coffee. The Roman numbers under the coffee tools indicate the beauty of time-old classics as well.
